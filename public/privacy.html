Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Created by Entler Entertainment

Overview

Gradient Clock is a simple, client-side web application that displays the current time through colorful, animated rings. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Gradient Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. The application:

  • Runs entirely in your web browser
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track your activity
  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not require user registration or login

Local Storage

The application uses browser local storage to save your preferences (theme and texture selections). This data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Gradient Clock does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

System Time Access

The application accesses your device's system time solely to display the clock. This information never leaves your device and is not stored or transmitted in any way.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact Entler Entertainment or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
