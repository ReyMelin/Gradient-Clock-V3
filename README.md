# Gradient-Clock-V3

A beautiful, customizable gradient clock with an intuitive three-step setup flow.

## Features

Your Gradient Clock now has a complete three-step setup flow:

1. **Select Theme** - Choose from 6 gradient themes (Metallic, Space, Dark, Flower Power, Winter, RGB)
2. **Select Texture** - Choose from 6 texture options (None, Roman Numerals, Dots, Numbers, Cardinal, Mixed Style) - all cards display your selected theme
3. **Clock Display** - View the final clock with a "Save my settings" button to persist your configuration

The settings are automatically saved to localStorage, so your chosen theme and texture will be remembered when you reload the page.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Select your preferred gradient theme from the landing page
3. Choose a texture style from the texture selection page
4. View your customized clock and click "Save my settings" to persist your preferences

## Customization

- **Themes**: 6 different gradient color schemes
- **Textures**: 6 different marker styles to display on the clock face
- **Responsive**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices
- **Persistent**: Your settings are saved locally and restored on reload
